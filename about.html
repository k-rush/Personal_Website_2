Founded in 2012, Street Diligence is an innovative analytics company that enables investment professionals to conduct more extensive due diligence and generate better investment ideas. Our proprietary platform provides unique analytical data not available elsewhere in the marketplace. We are based in New York City and led by experienced financial and technology entrepreneurs.

Management

Duo Ai – Partner and Technical Lead

Duo has a decade of software development experience in finance, most recently with Bridgewater Associates. Previously he worked at Goldman Sachs, Barclays Capital and Lehman Brothers. His prior entrepreneurial experience includes founding a foreign exchange trading start-up. Duo graduated from Dartmouth College with a B.A. in Computer Science and Economics and holds a CFA designation.

Erik Goshin – Product Management & Financial Analytics

Erik spent a decade at Capital IQ, a financial technology company, where he was a senior executive responsible for analytics and content. At Capital IQ, he built and managed teams in the US, Europe and India, as the business grew from a startup to over $300 million in revenue. Before Capital IQ, Erik spent five years as an investment banker serving the power and energy sector at Lehman Brothers and Chase.

Stephen Hazelton – Founder and CEO

Stephen has over fifteen years of experience in financial markets. He was most recently Managing Director of Savoy Capital, an investment firm and family office, where he led the firm’s investment activities. He is a second-time entrepreneur, having previously founded Stafford Group, a Vietnam-based brokerage firm still in operation today under new ownership. Stephen holds a B.A. from Yale University and an MBA from MIT Sloan, where he was a Peter Englander Fellow.

Chris Hontvet – Business Development

Chris has twelve years of experience in managing institutional investor relationships in the financial industry. Most recently, he worked in Investor Relations for Highfields Capital Management, a fundamental value, event-driven hedge fund. Prior to that, he worked in Marketing & Client Services for Satellite Asset Management, a multi-strategy hedge fund. He began his career in sales with UBS Alternative Investments. Chris graduated with a BA from Dartmouth College and holds the Chartered Alternative Investment Analyst (CAIA) designation.

Greg Warner – President, A.C. Israel Enterprises, Inc.

Greg joined A.C. Israel Enterprises, Inc. (aka ACIE and Ingleside Investors) in 1998 and is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the firm's investment and operating activities. ACIE is an investment firm that primarily represents the interests of the New York based Israel family. Before becoming President, Greg was a principal in the firm’s direct private equity group and was responsible for sourcing and overseeing numerous private equity investments. Prior to joining ACIE, Greg was a consultant for McKinsey & Company in its New York, Los Angeles and Hong Kong offices, advising companies in the financial services industry. Greg also spent four years in Marketing at IBM.

Greg currently serves as a Director of A.C. Israel Enterprises, Inc., Street Diligence and Gautier Steel and is on the advisory boards of Starwood Opportunity Fund VII, Rockwood Equity Partners and Fidelity Family Office Services. Greg serves on the boards of the Josiah Macy Foundation and Carnegie Hill Neighbors and is a member of the Young Presidents Organization (YPO), New York City Chapter.

Greg received a BS in Engineering from the University of California at San Diego, Cum Laude and Phi Beta Kappa, and an MBA from the Wharton School, with Distinction.

Alan Weber – Former Chairman & CEO, US Trust Co.; Former Vice Chairman, Aetna; Former Chairman, Citibank International

Alan Weber is the retired Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of US Trust Corporation. Prior to joining US Trust, Mr. Weber was Vice Chairman and Chief Financial Officer at Aetna Inc., where he was responsible for corporate strategy, capital management, information technology, e-business and financial operations.

Prior to his role with Aetna, Mr. Weber held a number of senior positions at Citibank N.A. for 27 years, including Chairman of Citibank International and Executive Vice President of Citibank. He also served as a member of Citibank’s Policy Committee. Mr. Weber is a member of the board of directors at three public companies: Diebold, Inc., a global provider of integrated self-service delivery systems, security and services; Broadridge Financial Solutions, Inc., a leading provider of investor communications, securities processing and clearing and outsourcing solutions to the financial services industry; and Sandridge Energy, Inc., an oil and gas exploration and production company. Mr. Weber is also on the boards of: OnForce Inc., a technology services marketplace, which Mr. Weber helped establish; and Chairman of KGS Alpha Capital Markets LLC, a fixed-income broker-dealer. Mr. Weber is also a member of the board of DCTV, a New York City-based charitable/service organization.

A native New Yorker, Mr. Weber received a BS in Economics from the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania, and an MBA from the Kellogg School at Northwestern University.
